Easy to care for

Dorper sheep are intelligent animals, which makes herding and working with them much easier

Hardy

They are a non selective feeder and will eat most grasses and weeds. They shed every year which eliminates shearing.

Meat

White Dorpers are fast growing sheep with a higher meat to bone ratio than most breeds.
